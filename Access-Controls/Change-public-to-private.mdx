---
title: "Change a Public Data Back to Private"
---

You can change the visibility of a public dataset, vendor or individual and make it private again at any time. However, before doing so, VoxDash checks whether the dataset meets certain conditions related to public access, referencing, and publishing history.

## Before You Change a Data to Private

A dataset, vendor or individual may not be fully removable from the public platform if any of the following conditions apply:

* The data has been referenced or used in other published datasets and has been assigned a DOI (Digital Object Identifier).
* The data has received more than 10 public views or downloads.
* The data has been publicly available for more than 7 days.

These conditions help preserve the integrity of published research, shared analytics, and external references that may already rely on the dataset.

## What Happens If These Conditions Apply?

If your data meets one or more of the conditions above:

* The data itself will remain accessible on the public platform.
* Only essential public metadata and required reference information will continue to be displayed.
* Sensitive or restricted content that is not required for public referencing will no longer be publicly visible.
* Existing DOI references and external citations will remain valid to prevent broken links or missing references in published work.

## Important Notes

* Changing a dataset from public to private does not remove existing citations or DOI records.
* Public metadata may include information such as the dataset title, description, publication date, author name, and DOI reference.
* If your dataset does not meet any of the conditions listed above, it can usually be converted back to private immediately.

## How to Change a Dataset to Private

1.**Locate the Public Access Column**  
 * **For Dataset**: Go to the [Projects](https://dataprovider.voxdash.com/projects) page and find the **Public Access** column (it is the ninth column from the left).  
 * **For Vendor**: Go to the [Vendors](https://dataprovider.voxdash.com/vendors) page and find the **Public Access** column (it is the ninth column from the left).  
 * **For Individual**: Go to the [Individuals](https://dataprovider.voxdash.com/individuals) page and find the **Public Access** column (it is the ninth column from the left).  

2.**Open the Editing Menu.**  
Click on the **arrow icon** next to the **Public Access** entry. This will open the editing menu, where you can adjust the access settings.

3.**Select Public Access Options**  
In the editing menu, change the dataset status from **Public** to **Private**. These options determine the level and type of access granted to the public.  

4.**Save Your Changes**  
Once you've selected the appropriate options, click the **Save Changes** button located in the bottom-right corner (the blue button) to apply your settings.
