1. [Overview](https://www.voxdash.com/TEST) 
2. [Data Profile](https://www.voxdash.com/vendor/1115/dataProfile)  
3. [Data Projects](https://www.voxdash.com/vendor/1115/datasets)

## **Overview**

In the [Overview section](https://www.voxdash.com/TEST), you can view essential information about the vendor, including:

* **Key Persons**: Important individuals involved in the organization or project, such as leaders or decision-makers.  
* **Institution Registration Number**: A unique identifier assigned to an institution when it is officially registered with relevant authorities.  
* **Institution Type**: The classification or category of the institution, such as public, private, nonprofit, etc.  
* **Vendor Identity in VoxDash**: A unique identifier used to recognize and manage vendors within the VoxDash platform.  
* **VAT Number**: A unique tax identification number assigned to a business for Value-Added Tax (VAT) purposes.  
* **Description**: A summary or explanation of the organization, product, or service.

## **Data Profiles**

Creating a **Data Profile** is the process of examining, analyzing, and creating useful summaries of data. This process offers a high-level overview that helps identify data quality issues, potential risks, and overall trends. Data profiling generates critical insights that companies can use to improve decision-making, optimize operations, and ensure data integrity. You can view all this information in the [Data Profile section](https://www.voxdash.com/vendor/1115/dataProfile)

## **Data Projects**

In the [Data Projects Section](https://www.voxdash.com/vendor/1115/datasets), you can access all data projects that the vendor is involved in. Simply click on a project to go to its specific project page, where you can find more detailed information and progress updates.

## **Network**

The [Network Section](https://www.voxdash.com/vendor/1115/network) showcases related data projects and dataset connections. This includes:

* **Related Data Projects**: Other projects that share similarities with the vendor's current initiatives.  
* **Dataset Network**: A network of common vendors, contributors, and interrelated project insights, helping you explore connections within the data ecosystem.
