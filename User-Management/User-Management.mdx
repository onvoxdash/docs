The **User Management page** in VoxDash enables organizations to manage access and permissions for their team members. From this page, administrators can add, edit, or remove users to maintain secure and efficient collaboration across projects.

Follow these steps to add a new team member to your organization:

1\. Click the Add User button located at the top-right corner of the page.

2\. In the pop-up form, enter the required details for the new user, such as their name, email address, and role (e.g., Administrator or Analyst).

3\. Click Add User to confirm and send the invitation.

Once submitted, an invitation email will automatically be sent to the new user, inviting them to join your organization in VoxDash.

When the invited user accepts the invitation and logs in, they will see your organization’s name displayed in the header, confirming that they have successfully joined your team.

## Managing Existing Users

After users have been added, you can manage their access or update their details directly from the User Management table.

 **Edit a User:** Scroll to the right of the user list and click the **More (⋮)**, then Select Edit to update their information or role.

 **Delete a User:** From the same More menu, select Delete to remove the user from your organization.

**Note:** Deleting a user will immediately revoke their access to all projects associated with your organization.

## Email Invitation Tips

If the invited user doesn’t receive their invitation email promptly, ask them to check their **Spam** folders.

You can also resend the invitation by returning to the User Management page and selecting Resend Invite from the More menu.

Managing users through this page ensures that the right people have access to the right data, helping maintain both efficiency and data security.

**Tip:** Make sure invited users check their spam or junk folders if they don’t receive the email promptly.

## Summary

By following these steps, you can easily add, manage, and organize your team members in VoxDash.
Proper user management ensures smoother collaboration, secure data sharing, and a better overall project workflow.
