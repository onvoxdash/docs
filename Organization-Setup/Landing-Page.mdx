Title: "Organization's Front Page"
---

The Organization Front Page section controls how your organization appears across VoxDash. It centralizes branding, identity, and attribution settings, including your logo, cover image, and a concise description of your organization and its role on the VoxDash landing page.

## steps to set your organization front page 

### 1.Set your organization logo

You can upload a logo or a symbolic graph for your organization to showcase it to your users.

### 2.Set your organization link

If your organization has a dedicated webpage, please enter the URL here.

### 3. Add detailed description

Provide a detailed description of the organization, including its purpose, scope, and key attributes. 

### 4. Add cover image

Here you can upload a cover image in your organization's front page showcase it to your users.

## **Additional Configuration Options**

When setting up your homepage, the following options are available (depending on your subscription plan):
* **Hide Staff Identity**  
Choose to anonymize the names of staff members who upload or manage datasets.

*  **Display Your Logo Alongside VoxDash Branding**  
Your organization's logo can appear next to the VoxDash logo for co-branding.

* **Enhanced Access and Features for Upgraded Plans**  
These customization options and privacy settings are available exclusively to users on higher-tier plans.

By following these steps you set your organization front page information on the VoxDash landing page right under the hero section.
