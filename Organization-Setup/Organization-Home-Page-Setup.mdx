Organizations or upgraded users in VoxDash can create a custom homepage for their company by setting up an organization domain. This feature enhances your brand visibility, allows for customization, and provides a professional digital presence within the VoxDash ecosystem.

## **What Is an Organization Domain?**

An **organization domain** is a unique subdomain (e.g., yourcompany.voxdash.com) representing your company or institution within VoxDash. It serves as a branded portal where visitors can view information, data contributions, and organizational identity.

### **Benefits of Setting an Organization Domain:**

* Enhances brand credibility and recognition  
* Establishes a centralized space for all your data activities  
* Provides a professional presence for public or internal users

## **Accessing Organization Domain**

To begin:

1. Open the **Data Provider Panel**.  
2. Go to the **Organization Page** from the main dashboard.  
3. Navigate to the **Organization Domain**
