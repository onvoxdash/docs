The Account Preference page allows you to manage and update your basic profile information within the platform. Keeping your profile up to date ensures accurate identification, improved collaboration, and a professional presence across the system.
This section explains each field and how to configure your account correctly.

## 1. Basic Profile Information

On this page, you can complete and edit your core profile details, including:

### 1.1 Title

Select or enter your professional title (for example: Dr., Mr., Ms., Prof., Manager).
Your title may appear in shared reports, project ownership details, and collaboration views.

### 1.2 Name

Enter your full name as you would like it displayed on:
- Shared datasets
- Reports
- Public project pages
- Internal collaboration views

Make sure your name is accurate and professionally formatted, as it may be visible to other users and external viewers (depending on your access settings).

### 1.3 VoxDash Unique URL

Your VoxDash Unique URL is your personalized profile link within the platform.
This URL:
- Identifies you within the system
- Can be shared with collaborators
- May appear on public datasets (if enabled)
- Enhances your professional visibility

**For best practice:**

- Use a clean, professional format (e.g., yourname, companyname, or firstname-lastname)
- Avoid special characters or unnecessary numbers
- Keep it consistent with your organization branding (if applicable)

Once saved, this URL becomes part of your professional presence on the platform.

## 2. Completing Your Full Profile

In addition to the basic information on the Account Preference page, you can further customize your profile by navigating to:
- Data Provider Settings
- Organization Settings
- Profile Setup Section

In these sections, you can:

- Add organization details
- Upload a profile picture or company logo
- Provide contact information
- Configure notification preferences
- Manage data visibility and sharing settings

Completing your full profile helps:

- Increase credibility when sharing datasets
- Improve collaboration within teams
- Enhance discoverability in analytics and eCommerce projects
- Build trust with external stakeholders

**Best Practices:**

- Keep your profile information accurate and updated.
- Use professional naming conventions.
- Ensure your unique URL aligns with your brand or organization.
- Review your settings periodically to maintain security and visibility preferences.

By properly configuring your Account Preference settings, you establish a strong foundation for managing projects, sharing data, and collaborating effectively within the platform.
