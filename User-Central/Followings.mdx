The **Followings** page allows you to manage all the items you are currently following within the platform. Following items helps you stay informed about updates, changes, trends, and new activity related to your interests in data analytics and eCommerce.

By following relevant entities, you can personalize your experience and receive more relevant insights across the platform.

## 1. What You Can Follow

You can follow various types of content and entities based on your professional needs and interests.

### 1.1 Data Projects

Follow specific data projects to:

* Receive updates when data is modified
* Track progress or completion status
* Monitor new reports or analytics outputs
* Stay informed about changes in access or metadata

This is useful when collaborating on projects or monitoring competitor datasets.

### 1.2 Vendors

Follow vendors to:

* Track newly published datasets
* Monitor vendor activity
* Stay updated on new analytics offerings
* Compare vendor performance over time

This is especially valuable in eCommerce environments where vendor performance analysis is critical.

### 1.3 Questions

Follow specific questions to:

* Track updates or responses
* Monitor discussions
* Stay informed about analytical inquiries related to your projects

This feature supports collaboration and knowledge sharing across teams.

### 1.4 Trends

Follow trends to:

* Monitor emerging market patterns
* Track industry performance
* Stay updated on analytical insights
* Identify growth opportunities

Trend tracking is particularly helpful for strategic decision-making in eCommerce and data-driven businesses.

### 1.5 Individuals

Follow other users to:

* Monitor their published projects
* Stay informed about their contributions
* Collaborate more effectively

This feature helps build professional connections within the platform.

### 1.6 Topics & Sub-Topics

Follow topics and sub-topics related to:

* Industry sectors
* Product categories
* Data methodologies
* Geographic regions
* Market segments

This ensures you receive relevant content aligned with your business or research focus.

### 1.7 Geo (Geographic Locations)

Follow specific geographic regions to:

* Track regional analytics
* Monitor country or market-level trends
* Analyze performance by location
* Compare regional datasets

This is particularly useful for global eCommerce and cross-market analysis.

## 2. Managing Your Followings

On the **Followings** tab, you can:

* View all entities you are currently following
* See categorized lists (Projects, Vendors, Trends, etc.)
* Click on any item to navigate directly to its dedicated page
* Unfollow items at any time

## To Unfollow:

1. Locate the item in your Followings list.
2. Click the **Unfollow** option.
3. The item will be removed from your Followings list.

Using the Followings feature helps you:

* Personalize your dashboard
* Reduce time spent searching for updates
* Stay informed about key analytics developments
* Monitor competitors or partners
* Make data-driven decisions faster

By actively managing your Followings, you create a customized monitoring system tailored to your data analytics and eCommerce goals.

The **Followings** page ensures you never miss important updates and helps you stay connected to the most relevant projects, trends, and entities within the platform.
