VoxDash offers powerful data analytics and ecommerce tools to help individuals and businesses collect, analyze, and act on survey data. While the **core features of VoxDash are free** to use, we also offer premium plans that unlock advanced capabilities for private users, small businesses, growing teams, and large enterprises.

Below is an overview of the key feature categories included across our plans:

• Secure cloud storage  
• Survey questions and response limits  
• Sample size capacity  
• Trended survey waves  
• User access points  
• Authorized domains  
• User groups  
• Data panel users  
• Data panel admins  
• AI prompt‑based survey Q&A  
• AI‑assisted open‑ended response coding  

Each plan offers a different level of capacity and functionality. The following sections summarize what each plan provides to help you choose the one that best fits your needs.

Below is a detailed breakdown of each plan to help you choose the one that best fits your needs:

## **Free Plan — Ideal for Personal Use or Small-Scale Projects**

The Free Plan provides access to basic features that are suitable for individual users, students, or anyone running small, non-commercial projects.

Not Included in the Free Plan:  
• AI-powered prompt-based analysis  
• AI-assisted open-ended response coding  
• Advanced user management  

## **Starter Plan — For Small Businesses Ready to Grow** 

The Starter Plan is designed for early-stage companies or teams that need more capacity and access to AI-driven insights. 

## **Pro Plan — For Growing Teams Requiring Advanced Features** 

The Pro Plan supports expanding teams with a higher volume of data, more user roles, and powerful enterprise features. 

## **Enterprise Plan — Tailored for Large-Scale Operations** 

The Enterprise Plan is fully customizable for large businesses or organizations that require bespoke analytics infrastructure, extended AI capabilities, and organization-wide user management.


## Plan Management

You can compare all plans at any time to decide which option best suits your needs. You may upgrade or downgrade your plan directly from your account settings. All subscriptions renew monthly and are billed automatically to ensure uninterrupted access.

## Add‑Ons for the Pro Plan

The Pro Plan offers a rich set of tools for teams that need advanced survey analytics and e‑commerce insights. However, some organizations may require additional capacity for specific features without upgrading to the Enterprise Plan.

To support growing data needs, VoxDash provides optional feature-specific add‑ons. These allow Pro Plan users to purchase extra capacity—such as additional survey waves, more storage, or increased sample limits—only where they need it, offering flexibility as your usage scales.
