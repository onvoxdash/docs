The **Pro Plan** provides a comprehensive set of features tailored for growing teams that need advanced data analytics and e-commerce tools. However, every business is different, and your data needs may grow beyond the standard limits provided in the Pro Plan.

To help you scale **on-demand**, VoxDash offers **feature-specific add-ons**. These add-ons allow Pro Plan users to purchase additional capacity for specific features without upgrading to an Enterprise Plan.

## **Why Use Add-ons?**

Add-ons are ideal when:

* You’re running a high-volume survey campaign and need more storage or sample size.
* Your organization grows and requires more user access points or domains.
* You need additional AI credits for deep, prompt-based data analysis.

**Note** Add-ons allow you to stay on the Pro Plan while extending only the features you need.

## Add‑On Categories

### Data Governance

#### Authorized Domains

Increase the maximum number of authorized email domains that can access your data across all projects.

* Useful for teams working with multiple brands, departments, or client organizations.

#### User Access Points

Expand the maximum number of unique user email addresses that can access your projects.

* Recommended for growing teams with more collaborators, analysts, or stakeholders.

### Organization Management

#### User Groups

Increase the number of user groups you can create across all data projects.

* Helpful for organizations that need separate workspaces or permissions for different departments or teams.

#### Data Panel Users

Add more standard users to your data provider panel.

* These users can view data, participate in workflows, and support survey operations.

#### Data Panel Admins

Increase the number of admin‑level or organizer users with advanced permissions in the data provider panel.

* Suitable for organizations that require multiple panel managers or supervisors.

### AI Integration

#### AI (ChatGPT) Credits

Purchase additional one‑time AI credits used for ChatGPT‑powered processing.

Credits may be used for:

• AI prompt‑based Q&A

• Automated coding of open‑ended responses

• Analytical summaries and insights

### Survey Management

#### Survey Trended Waves

Increase the maximum number of trended waves your surveys can have across all projects.

* Useful for teams conducting recurring surveys or tracking changes over time.

#### Survey Questions

Expand the total number of survey questions allowed across your data projects.

* Ideal for larger or more complex questionnaires.

#### Survey Sample Size

Increase the maximum sample size you can collect across all projects.

* Recommended for research‑heavy teams, agencies, or high‑volume survey deployments.
