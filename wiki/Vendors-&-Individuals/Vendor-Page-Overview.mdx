The **Vendor Page** provides detailed information about a specific vendor. It includes the following five main sections:

1. **Overview**  
2. **Data Profiles**  
3. **Data Projects**  
4. **Network**

## **Overview**

In the **Overview** section, you can view essential information about the vendor, including:

* **Key Persons**: Important individuals involved in the organization or project, such as leaders or decision-makers.  
* **Institution Registration Number**: A unique identifier assigned to an institution when it is officially registered with relevant authorities.  
* **Institution Type**: The classification or category of the institution, such as public, private, nonprofit, etc.  
* **Vendor Identity in VoxDash**: A unique identifier used to recognize and manage vendors within the VoxDash platform.  
* **VAT Number**: A unique tax identification number assigned to a business for Value-Added Tax (VAT) purposes.  
* **Description**: A brief summary or explanation of the organization, product, or service.

## **Data Profiles**

**Data profiling** is the process of examining, analyzing, and creating useful summaries of data. This process offers a high-level overview that helps identify data quality issues, potential risks, and overall trends. Data profiling generates critical insights that companies can use to improve decision-making, optimize operations, and ensure data integrity.

## **Data Projects**

In the **Data Projects** section, you can access all data projects that the vendor is involved in. Simply click on a project to be navigated to its specific project page, where you can find more detailed information and progress updates.

## **Network**

The **Network** section showcases related data projects and dataset connections. This includes:

* **Related Data Projects**: Other projects that share similarities with the vendor's current initiatives.  
* **Dataset Network**: A network of common vendors, contributors, and interrelated project insights, helping you explore connections within the data ecosystem.
